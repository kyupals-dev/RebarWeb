General Information

Rebar Vista is an AI-powered system that measures concrete column volumes and estimates cement mixtures for Philippine residential construction. It uses Mask R-CNN computer vision to analyze rebar structures and calculate precise measurements.

Key Benefits:

  • Eliminates Manual Errors: AI reduces human mistakes in volume calculations
  • Reduces Material Waste: Accurate measurements prevent overordering
  • Cost-Effective: Affordable for small to large contractors
  • Instant Results: Real-time calculations and cement mixture recommendations
  • Standalone Operation: Works without internet on construction sites

What Rebar Vista Can Do:

  • AI Volume Measurement: Mask R-CNN technology detects and measures rebar structures
  • Cement Mixture Calculation: Provides cement:sand:aggregates ratios based on measured volumes
  • Single-Shot Analysis: One image per column
  • Philippine Standards: Optimized for local construction practices and materials

Compatible With:

  • Residential buildings: 1-2 stories only
  • Square columns up to 3.5 meters high
  • Philippine residential construction standards

Problems It Solves:

  • Manual calculation errors leading to material waste
  • Inaccurate cement mixtures causing structural issues
  • Time-consuming manual measurement processes
  • High cost of advanced measurement equipment

Technology Used:

  • Mask R-CNN AI: Advanced neural network for precise rebar detection
  • Raspberry Pi Hardware: Portable unit with integrated high-resolution camera
  • Local Processing: On-site processing eliminates internet dependency
  • Computer Vision: Analyzes single images to segment rebar structures

System Limitations:

  • Building Types: Only 1-2 story residential homes
  • Column Shapes: Only square columns (no spiral, circular, L-shape, rectangular, or T-shape)
  • Height Limit: Columns up to 3.5 meters only
  • Structure Types: Only columns (no beams, walls, slabs, foundations)
  • Integration: Does not connect to other construction management systems
  • Environment: Not suitable for extreme weather conditions

How to Use Rebar Vista

Simple 5-Step Process:

  1. Setup: Power on Rebar Vista and position near your column (square, up to 3.5m)
  2. Capture: Point camera at complete rebar cage with good lighting and take one photo
  3. AI Analysis: Wait 10-15 seconds for Mask R-CNN to detect and segment rebar sections
  4. Results: Review volume calculations and cement mixture recommendations
  5. Apply: Use measurements for material ordering and follow provided ratios

Why It Works:

  • Local processing ensures reliability on construction sites
  • Philippine-optimized for local construction practices
  • Single-shot efficiency saves time per column
  • Proven Mask R-CNN accuracy in testing

For Optimal Performance:

  • Good Lighting: Use natural daylight when possible
  • Clear View: Ensure entire rebar cage is visible without obstructions
  • Proper Distance: Position 2-4 feet from column
  • Clean Environment: Remove concrete debris blocking rebar visibility
  • Stable Positioning: Keep system steady when capturing images

Quality Control:

  • Take measurements only from the front phase angle
  • Compare results with manual estimates for validation
  • Document measurement conditions for project records
  • Keep camera lens clean for accurate detection

If System Isn't Working:

  • Wrong Column Type: Verify column is square and under 3.5m
  • Poor Detection: Improve lighting or remove obstructions
  • Slow Processing: Normal wait time is 10-15 seconds
  • Power Issues: Check power supply and restart system
  • Camera Problems: Clean lens and check connections

If Results Look Incorrect:

  • Inconsistent Numbers: Retake photo only the front phase angle
  • Unrealistic Values: Ensure entire column is in frame
  • Detection Errors: Verify good lighting and clear rebar visibility

Environmental Challenges:

  • Dust/Debris: Clean camera lens regularly
  • Vibrations: Use stable platform away from machinery
  • Weather: Operate in shaded areas during extreme heat
  • Storage: Ensure adequate space for image processing

Remember System Limits:

  • Only for Philippine residential buildings (1-2 stories)
  • Works independently without internet
  • Cannot connect to other construction software
  • Designed specifically for local construction standards
